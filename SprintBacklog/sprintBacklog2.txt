============================================================
SPRINT 2 - POWER CARDS AND SPECIAL ACTIONS
==========================================

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT2.001
OWNER    : power-cards
NAME     : Power card activation rules
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 4 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Power cards are cards with value 7 or higher.
* Power effects activate ONLY if:
  a) the card is drawn from the deck, AND
  b) the card is immediately discarded.
* Power cards drawn from the discard pile do NOT activate.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT2.002
OWNER    : power-cards
NAME     : Look at your own card
PRIORITY : MEDIUM
ESTIMATE : 2 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Player may peek at one of their own face-down cards.
* Card must be returned face down after viewing.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT2.003
OWNER    : power-cards
NAME     : Look at opponent's card
PRIORITY : MEDIUM
ESTIMATE : 2 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Player may peek at one face-down card belonging to another player.
* Only the activating player gains this information.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT2.004
OWNER    : power-cards
NAME     : Blind trade ability
PRIORITY : MEDIUM
ESTIMATE : 4 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Player may swap any two face-down cards on the table.
* No card may be viewed before or after the swap.
* Cards may belong to any players.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT2.005
OWNER    : power-cards
NAME     : Informed trade (King / 13)
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 6 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Player may look at:
  a) one of their own cards, AND
  b) one opponent's card.
* Player then chooses whether to swap the two cards.
