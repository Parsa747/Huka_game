============================================================
SPRINT 1 - CORE GAMEPLAY RULES
==============================

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT1.001
OWNER    : gameplay
NAME     : Initial card dealing and table layout
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 1 day
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Deal exactly 4 cards to each player.
* All cards are face down.
* Cards must be arranged in a 2x2 square in front of each player.
* No player may view cards at this stage.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT1.002
OWNER    : gameplay
NAME     : Memorization phase (3-2-1 Look)
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 4 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* All players count down together: "3, 2, 1, Look".
* Each player may look at ONLY their bottom two cards.
* Cards may be viewed once and must be returned face down.
* No discussion or signaling is allowed.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT1.003
OWNER    : gameplay
NAME     : Turn-based draw system
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 1 day
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Turns proceed clockwise.
* On a turn, a player may draw from:
  a) the draw pile, OR
  b) the top of the discard pile.
* Only one card may be drawn per turn.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT1.004
OWNER    : gameplay
NAME     : Swap drawn card with face-down card
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 6 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Player may replace any one of their face-down cards
  with the drawn card.
* The replaced card is immediately discarded face up.
* Player may not look at the replaced card before discarding.

---

---

TYPE     : FEATURE
ID       : FEAT1.005
OWNER    : gameplay
NAME     : Immediate discard option
PRIORITY : HIGH
ESTIMATE : 3 hours
STATUS   : Planned
DETAILS  :

* Player may discard the drawn card without swapping.
* No other action is required unless the card is a power card.

---
